eLend: eBook Lending for the Digital Age

Responsible eBook Lending with Stellar blockchain & Soroban Smart Contracts

Introduction

Welcome to the eLend project website. eLend is a methodology that utilizes blockchain & smart contract technologies to allow libraries to responsibly lend ebooks while respecting copyright law and rights of the content authors. Our proposed solution for ebook lending utilizes the Stellar blockchain for tokenizing ebooks and the Soroban smart contract platform to define a contract that ensures loaned ebooks are only readable by one person. This is accomplished by generating a token when a book is checked out and then delivering an token encrypted ebook file to the user that can only be decrypted with the users address and token.

From here you can read our proposal for responsible ebook lending, check out an overview of the technology stack, review the process workflow and more. Feel free to look around and email us at elendteam@cdr2.com with any questions or just use our Contact Form.

Objectives

  • Develop a system to manage eBook lending using Stellar blockchain.
  • Ensure that eBooks can only be checked out by one person at a time.
  • Revoke access to eBooks once they are returned or the lending period expires.
  • Utilize Soroban smart contracts to automate and secure the lending process.
